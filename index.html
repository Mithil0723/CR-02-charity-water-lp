Every person deserves access to life's most basic need

771 million people lack access to clean water. That's nearly 1 in 10 people worldwide. The majority live in isolated rural areas and spend hours every day walking to collect water for their families.

Not only does walking for water keep children out of school or take up time that parents could be using to earn money, but the water often carries diseases that can make whole communities sick.

But access to clean water means education, income and health - especially for women and children.
